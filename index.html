Why Wonde

Work-life balance

Wonde is a fast-paced business, driven to excel, but we also know the importance of separating work from our personal lives. We are committed to balance. We focus on productivity rather than hours. We run round the block togheter. We take time out to sit and have a lunch.

We give back

We believe that doing good is the right thing to do. We foster strong community partnerships, encouraging volunteering days and operate a great CSR programme.

We value our employees

We value, support and champion those we work with. We promote personal growth and happiness since our succes is dependent on our collective energy, intelligence and contributions of all our team members.

We protect you

Wonde has a comprehensive employee benefit scheme incluiding: pension contributions and contributory holiday scheme. We ensure your wellbeing is a priority through opportunities to learn, to be active, to give to others & pay attention to the present moment.

We build a culture of conservation

We work to reduce the enviromental impacto of Wonde through recycling schemes, telecommuting, reducing paper waste and ensuring we turn off the lights at the end of the day.

We are a team

Wonde works as one, across geographical boundaries, to ensure each team member is supported, encouraged and treaten with respect and integrity. Working collaboratively, we are focused on a single vision, and strive to excel at that vision.
